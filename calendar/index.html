Intensive weekend training to orient prospective members on the basic skills required to operate effectively in search and rescue operations. Intended for people with no prior SAR experience. Navigation *, TBA Panek May 18
Navigation training and signoffs, concentrating on map and compass, gps and caltopo usage. Focusing on becoming an effective nav lead during operations. Prerequisite include ability to read topo maps and relate terrain features to topo maps.
Gear/Truck Familiarization *, TBA Barkley May 19
An introduction to our extensive gear collection and where everything is stored in the trucks. Learn how to set up a mobile command post.
Personal Rock Skills *, TBA Gleason Jun 22
This two day course covers general rock skills skill stations, scenarios, and discussion of their application within the SAR community. Topics include anchors (trad gear and natural protection), belaying, rappelling, basic self-rescue, jugging/ascending, and personal emergency evac equipment with scenarios. Focus will be on both theory and practice. Personal rock skills is a prerequisite for Basic Technical Training.
Tracking * G, TBA Dacre Jul 20
The tracking training will cover primarily basic topics in tracking - recognizing sign, preserving clues, the structure of a tracking team, and following sign. Guests are welcome at this training, pending leader approval.
Low Angle *, TBA Lariviere Jul 21
We will focus on single line rescue systems as an introduction to the larger world of technical rescue. Personal Rock Skills is required to attend this training.
DO Skills *, Redwood City Barkley Jul 31
We will cover the theory of being Duty Officer (what needs to be done during any DO shift, DO-ing during a training, what needs to be done in the ramp up for a search, etc), and the practice of DO-ing (how do we accomplish all of these things with the tools we have, like bamru.net). For new and experienced DOs alike!
AHC Skills *, TBA Barkley Aug 14
The second half of the DO/AHC skills night series. This teaches you how to be an At-Home Coordinator of an operation.
Basic Technical Rescue *, TBA Allen Aug 24
Basic Technical Rescue is two full days, covering the basics of our standard two-line TTRS high-angle rescue system. Participants must have completed Personal Rock Skills and Low Angle.
Search Management, TBA Kantarjiev Sep 21
We will spend two days planning and executing the first operational period of a search operation, in slow motion, in rugged terrain.
Operations Leader (OL) Training, County Room 101, 455 County Center, Redwood City, CA Chang Oct 12
Participation by invitation only. For Field and Technical Members.
