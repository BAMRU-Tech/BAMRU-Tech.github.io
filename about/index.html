About the Team

The Bay Area Mountain Rescue Unit is an all-volunteer wilderness search and rescue (SAR) unit, based in the San Francisco Bay Area. BAMRU is an accredited member of the Mountain Rescue Association and is a FEMA and California OES Type I search and rescue resource.

Our History

The unit has been an independent, 501(c)3 non-profit corporation for over 35 years. We are proud of our affiliation with the San Mateo County (California) Sheriff's Emergency Services Bureau.

Our Volunteers

BAMRU members are experienced backpackers, climbers, mountaineers, and skiers. Our experience allows us to work safely and comfortably in challenging and hazardous wilderness situations. We come from all over the Bay Area, from Marin to Santa Cruz and from San Francisco to Contra Costa County.

Our Credentials

As a Mountain Rescue Association (MRA) accredited team, BAMRU must undergo annual recertification in rock rescue, winter operations, and general search techniques. We are a California OES Type 1 resource, which means we are ready to help anyone on any terrain in California.
