Meet the Team

Our monthly general meetings are open to the public, generally from 7:30-9:30pm on the third Monday of the month (except where that falls on a holiday). Guests are encouraged to attend our monthly General Meetings. People with particularly strong outdoors experience, prior SAR experience, or a compelling desire to be of service to the community are especially welcome.


Check the calendar

Meeting Locations

The meeting alternates between two locations, one in the Peninsula and the other in the East Bay. Please check the calendar to find out where the next meeting is taking place.

Peninsula Meeting

Our Peninsula meetings are in Redwood City in either of two adjacent buildings (455 County Center Room 101, or 400 County Center in the San Mateo County Hall of Justice and Records, Jury Assembly Room in the basement). Look for a BAMRU member in a red uniform jacket for access (37.488584, -122.23053). Please note that after the meeting begins, you may not be able to get in. Convenient parking is available at the County Garage (400 Middlefield Rd).

East Bay Meeting

Our East Bay meeting takes place at the East Bay Region Parks Public Safety Headquarters (Nike Classroom) near 17930 Lake Chabot Road, Castro Valley (37.720672, -122.096198). Parking is available outside the building.
