Meet the Team

Our monthly general meetings are open to the public, generally from 7:30-9:30pm on the third Monday of the month (except where that falls on a holiday). Guests are encouraged to attend our monthly General Meetings. People with particularly strong outdoors experience, prior SAR experience, or a compelling desire to be of service to the community are especially welcome.


Check the calendar

Meeting Locations

The meeting are currently hybrid between in person in Redwood City and Zoom. Please check the calendar to find out when the next meeting is taking place.

In Person Meeting

The in person portion of our meetings are held in Redwood City in the San Mateo County Regional Operations Center. Look for a BAMRU member in a red uniform jacket around the glass doors on the east side of the building for access (37.489357, -122.230445). Please note that after the meeting begins, you may not be able to get in. Convenient parking is available at the County Garage (400 Middlefield Rd).

Online Meeting

An email with a Zoom invite link is sent out before every meeting. Please reach out to recruiting@bamru.org to receive these invitations.
